Unlocking value creation & exchange, powered by AI.

Today's models learned the world secondhand, from text, images, and recordings of things other people made. They can describe a game but they have never shipped one. They can summarise a live economy but they have never run one.

Interactive content is different. It is made, operated, and monetised in an unbroken loop between a creator, their tools, and the people who play. To build for it, a model needs a harness to natively craft that loop, not a transcript of it.
